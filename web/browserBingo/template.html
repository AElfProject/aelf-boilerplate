Game Rules

The current block height and user's seed will be used to calculate a random number between [0, 255]. And we will get SMALL, MIDDLE or BIG.
1. You can bet SMALL or BIG.
2. The contract always wins when getting MIDDLE.
3. No more than 50 bets in a time.
